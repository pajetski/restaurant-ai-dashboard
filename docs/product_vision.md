# DUDE Product Vision

## Product name
DUDE

## Meaning
Data. Understanding. Direction. Execution.

## Product definition
DUDE is an AI operations platform designed to help businesses turn operational data into actionable insight and follow-through.

DUDE starts with restaurant operations and is being designed to support broader operations-heavy businesses over time.

## Core purpose
DUDE exists to:
- ingest messy business data from real-world systems
- normalize that data into usable operational models
- identify issues, patterns, and opportunities
- generate direction through alerts, forecasts, and recommendations
- support execution through workflows and operational follow-through

## Restaurant-first scope
The first product wedge is restaurant operations, including:
- receiving PDF intake
- spreadsheet and Excel import
- Toast-first POS integration
- inventory and vendor normalization
- menu change awareness
- business hours change awareness
- scheduling bottleneck detection
- historical-data-based inventory prediction
- ordering recommendations
- operational alerts and manager summaries

## Product principles
- Build a real software system, not a thin wrapper around an LLM
- Prefer durable architecture over quick hacks
- Favor deterministic business logic first, AI second
- Design for modular expansion
- Focus on measurable business value

## Long-term vision
DUDE should evolve into a professional operations platform that helps managers and operators understand what is happening, what will likely happen next, what action should be taken, and how to execute on it efficiently.
